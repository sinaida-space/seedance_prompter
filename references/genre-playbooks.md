# Genre Playbooks

Each playbook: the default grammar (camera / light / grade / texture / pace) plus one
full ready-to-adapt example prompt. Adapt the location, character, palette, and beats
to the actual idea — never paste an example back unedited.

## Product / Commercial

Camera: static locked-off or slow push in. No handheld, no whip pan.
DoF: shallow — subject sharp, background soft bokeh.
Light: one key + rim light, practical highlight on the product.
Grade: clean neutral base, light warm kiss. No heavy grading.
Texture: reflective surface under the product, minimal background.
Pace: slow — 8-10s reveal, 4-5s final hold.

```text
Format: 16:9

Visual style: ultra-clean digital cinema, IMAX quality, no film grain. Color palette: pure white highlights, soft warm ivory midtones, deep charcoal shadows. Brushed stainless steel surface beneath product with soft directional reflection.

Location: minimalist studio environment. White cyclorama background, seamless. Single rectangular softbox from camera-left at 45 degrees. Subtle rim light from behind-right.

[0:00-5s] Static locked-off, macro lens. Product centered in frame. Slow reveal of surface detail — texture, finish, edges. Light catches rim.
[5s-10s] Imperceptibly slow push in to hero angle. Product fills 60% of frame. Highlight sweeps across surface.
[10s-15s] Hold on hero frame. Slight bokeh shift on background. Breathe in light.

Stable face throughout. No morphing. No deformation. No flickering. No ghosting. Realistic physics. 4K cinematic.
```

## Fashion / Beauty

Camera: orbital shot for full-look presentation, slow push in for face/beauty, tracking for movement.
Light: fashion = hard rim + soft fill; beauty = large soft source, minimal shadow.
Grade: high-key overexposed editorial or warm golden hour. Never flat/dull.
Texture: fabric (velvet, silk, structured weave), floor surface.

```text
@Image1 — full-body reference for the model. Preserve exact likeness, proportions, and face throughout.

Format: 9:16 vertical

Visual style: high-end fashion editorial, 35mm Kodak Portra 400 emulation. Color palette: warm ivory skin tones, deep forest green clothing accents, dusty rose in background bokeh, charcoal shadows. Subtle film grain.

Location: rooftop terrace, late afternoon golden hour. Warm amber side light from camera-right horizon. Pale limestone surface underfoot. Glass and steel architecture soft in background bokeh.

Character: Use @Image1 as strict identity reference. Preserve exact likeness throughout. No beautification. No deformation. Wearing: oversized structured blazer, deep forest green, single-button, sharp lapels. Wide-leg trousers, cream, high waist. Hair swept back.

[0:00-4s] Slow push in from full body to waist. Model stands still, facing camera. Wind moves blazer lapel slightly.
[4s-9s] Orbital shot — camera arcs from front to three-quarter view. Model turns head slowly to follow. Fabric moves.
[9s-15s] Slow pull back to full body. Model takes one step forward. Final frame: full silhouette against golden sky bokeh.

Stable face throughout. No morphing. No deformation. No flickering. No ghosting. Realistic physics. 4K cinematic.
```

## Sci-fi / Cyberpunk / Fantasy

Camera: drone descend for world reveal, low angle/Dutch for power, crane down.
Light: multiple colored practicals (neon, hologram, bioluminescence), hard rim.
Grade: neon cyberpunk (magenta/cyan) or cold desaturated blue with one warm practical.
Texture: wet surfaces for neon reflection, metal grating, holographic glass.
FX vocabulary: `volumetric light beams`, `particle dust`, `lens flare from practical neon`.

```text
Format: 16:9

Visual style: near-future cyberpunk, anamorphic lens, oval bokeh on background lights. Color palette: deep cyan in shadows, magenta-pink neon reflections, cold blue-white midtones, isolated warm amber from single practical. No film grain — clean digital cinema.

Location: rain-soaked elevated platform, megacity at night, 40 stories above street. Wet metal grating underfoot with cyan neon reflections. Holographic advertising panels soft in background. Volumetric mist at knee level. Heavy rain — droplet physics visible in light beams.

Character: tall, sharp-featured, dark complexion, close-cropped natural hair, lean build. Wearing: long asymmetric black technical trench coat with integrated lighting strips at shoulder seams emitting faint blue light. Preserve exact likeness. No deformation.

[0:00-5s] Drone descend from above city canopy — slow, majestic. City scale revealed. Character visible as small figure on platform below.
[5s-11s] Low Dutch angle close on character face and chest. Neon reflects on wet coat. Volumetric rain passes through light beam behind character.
[11s-15s] Static locked-off. Character turns head to look at camera. Holds. Neon sign behind them pulses once. Cut to black.

Stable face throughout. No morphing. No deformation. No flickering. No ghosting. Realistic physics. 4K cinematic.
```

## Music Video / Reels

Camera: cut on the beat, whip pan transitions, mix handheld and locked-off.
Pace: each beat 2-4s, tie rhythm explicitly to `@Audio1`.
Grade: high saturation or high-contrast black and white — never neutral.
Audio line: `Video rhythm references @Audio1's beats. Cut on downbeat.`

```text
@Image1 — full-body reference for the performer. Use @Image1 as strict identity reference.
@Audio1 — music track reference. Video rhythm references @Audio1's beats. Cut on downbeat.

Format: 9:16 vertical

Visual style: high-energy music video, vivid saturated colors, subtle 35mm grain. Color palette: deep electric blue in shadows, hot coral-pink in highlights, yellow-gold practical lighting from below, white LED strip accents. High contrast.

Location: empty industrial warehouse, night. Concrete floor with standing water reflections. Exposed steel beams. Multiple colored LED strip lights along walls: electric blue and coral. Smoke machine haze at floor level.

Character: Use @Image1 as strict identity reference. Preserve exact likeness throughout. No beautification. Wearing: oversized white cropped hoodie, black baggy cargo pants, white chunky sneakers. Hair loose, natural texture.

[0:00-2s] Static locked-off, low angle. Beat drop — character snaps into movement.
[2s-5s] Handheld tracking — follows character lateral movement. Smoke swirls.
[5s-9s] Whip pan transition. Close on face. Frame-accurate lip sync from @Audio1.
[9s-12s] Wide locked-off. Full choreography. Beat-sync cuts.
[12s-15s] Crash zoom to face. Character looks into lens. Pulse of blue neon from behind.

Stable face throughout. No morphing. No deformation. No flickering. No ghosting. Realistic physics. 4K cinematic.
```

## Horror / Thriller

Camera: handheld shake to build dread, slow push in for tension, static hold for the reveal of a threat.
Pace: slow build — 8-10s setup, 3-5s reveal. Never rush.
Light: one practical source (flashlight, phone, cracked window), deep shadows everywhere.
Texture: crumbling plaster, mold, wet stone, rusted metal.
Moderation: avoid blood/violence — use threat, implication, shadow instead.

```text
Format: 16:9

Visual style: psychological horror, 16mm heavy grain, high contrast. Color palette: near-black shadows with zero detail, sickly desaturated green midtones from single fluorescent source, skin tones pale and cool. Bleach bypass look.

Location: abandoned hospital corridor, night. Cracked plaster walls with peeling institutional green paint. Broken ceiling tiles. Water-stained concrete floor. Single flickering fluorescent tube at far end of corridor. Deep shadow in foreground.

Character: young woman, early 20s, pale skin, dark under-eyes, dark hair tangled. Wearing: hospital gown, white, short-sleeved. Bare feet. Facing away from camera at start. Preserve exact likeness. No deformation.

[0:00-6s] Static locked-off, low angle, wide. Character as small figure far end of corridor, back to camera. Fluorescent flickers. She does not move.
[6s-11s] Imperceptibly slow push in. Shadow grows in foreground. Something enters frame edge — just a shape, no clear form.
[11s-15s] Handheld slight shake. Character turns head slowly toward camera over shoulder. Her expression: not fear — recognition. Hold.

Stable face throughout. No morphing. No deformation. No flickering. No ghosting. Realistic physics. 4K cinematic.
```

## Documentary / Reportage

Camera: handheld, never stabilized. Observational — camera reacts to the subject, not choreographed.
Light: natural/available only. Mixed color temperatures are welcome, not a flaw.
Grade: minimal, slightly desaturated, neutral skin tones. Authenticity over polish.
Style line: `observational documentary style. Camera reacts to subject, not choreographed.`

## Viral Hook (Reels/TikTok)

Hook: the first 2 seconds MUST contain movement, a reveal, or a visual surprise — write this explicitly as its own beat.
Format: 9:16 only, fill the vertical frame completely.
Pace: faster than any other genre — 2-3s beats.
Grade: vivid, saturated, high contrast. Flat/neutral kills engagement.
Text-safe zones: keep the top 20% and bottom 30% of frame visually simple for overlay text.

```text
Format: 9:16 vertical

Visual style: high-energy viral content, vivid oversaturated grade, slight vignette. Color palette: electric turquoise dominant, hot yellow accent, pure white highlights, deep black crush. No grain — crisp digital.

Location: rooftop pool at sunset, luxury building. Still water surface with saturated sky reflection. White pool edge. City skyline soft in background bokeh.

[0:00-2s] HOOK: Crash zoom from wide to close — instant energy. Subject enters frame from below water level — surprise reveal.
[2s-5s] Tracking shot following subject along pool edge. Camera at water level. Saturated reflections in foreground.
[5s-9s] Static overhead. Subject looks up at camera. Visual pause — contrast to energy.
[9s-13s] Snap to real-time from slight slow motion. Whip pan reveal of city view. Scale hit.
[13s-15s] Hold on wide — subject and city. Loop-ready end frame.

Stable face throughout. No morphing. No deformation. No flickering. No ghosting. Realistic physics. 4K cinematic.
```

## UGC / Phone Realism

Use when the brief calls for "not cinematic, feels leaked/real":
`Shot on phone, no color grade, no stabilization, natural grain, auto-exposure flickering, slight motion blur. Not cinematic. Not polished. Real.`

Variant, old/leaked footage: `Looks like filmed on an old Nokia or cheap Android, low quality, grainy, slightly overexposed, handheld shake. Raw and authentic. No filters.`

Variant, GoPro: `GoPro wide angle aesthetic. Slight barrel distortion. Auto-exposure adjusting. No stabilization. Raw authentic footage feel.`
